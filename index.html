Why I Love Festivals

Music festivals are my favorite. I wish I could live at one. Below is a link for more general info about festivals.
To be brief, music festivals are usually large gatherings of people to celebrate life, music and the arts. The ones
I go to are usually 2-5 days long. Festivals are a great way to meet new people from all over the world as people
travel great lengths to attend these events.
